We Are Nurses First. Entrepreneurs Second.

The Origin Story

format_quote

"Watching my mother navigate the complexities of managing her hypertension was a wake-up call. The anxiety of tracking readings, the fragmented communication with her doctor, and the sheer burden of chronic illness felt overwhelming. As a nurse, I knew the system. As a son, I felt the gap. PressureYangu was born from the desire to bridge that gap—to provide clinical precision wrapped in compassionate, accessible design."

OL

Okumu Lozete

Founder & CEO
